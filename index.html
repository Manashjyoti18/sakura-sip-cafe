Our Story

Born from a love of anime, coffee, and community.

A Dreamy Escape 🌸

Sakura Sip Café was born in 2024 from a simple dream: to create a space where anime fans, foodies, and coffee lovers could come together in a magical setting inspired by Japanese culture and animation.

Every corner of our café is designed to transport you into an anime world — from our hand-painted murals to our character-themed menu items. Our baristas are trained in latte art that would make Studio Ghibli proud!
